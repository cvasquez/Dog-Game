Changelog

Dig notes from the underground

v0.5.1 2026-04-03
Shop Town
Changed
  • All shops and the Stash Box are now grouped together in a little town at the surface, sitting on a continuous undiggable platform
v0.5.0 2026-04-03
Stash Box & Death Penalty
Added
  • Stash Box on the surface — deposit resources to protect them from death and recall penalties
  • Death now causes loss of all carried resources — bank your loot to keep it safe
Fixed
  • Landing after a fall no longer drains stamina — only HP damage and brief stun remain
v0.4.1 2026-04-03
Fall Damage Rework
Balance
  • Fall damage is now distance-based instead of velocity-based — no damage for falls of 2 blocks or less
  • Falls of 3+ blocks deal progressive damage that scales quadratically with distance
  • Fall damage now only affects HP, no longer drains stamina
v0.4.0 2026-04-03
Survival Update: HP, Hazards, Darkness & Prestige
Added
  • Health system with breed-specific HP (Pitty tankiest, Terrier most fragile)
  • HP bar in the HUD that regenerates slowly when grounded
  • Fall damage now reduces HP instead of stamina — high falls can kill
  • Crumbling tiles that break after being stood on — watch your step over gaps
  • Ambient darkness vignette that intensifies the deeper you dig
  • Depth-based camera zoom — field of view narrows underground for claustrophobic tension
  • Prestige system — buy all upgrades to reset for a permanent +8% all stats per level
  • Achievement system with 25 achievements across exploration, collection, progression, prestige, and survival
  • Achievement popup notifications with gold border styling
  • Achievements tab in the shop with stats summary
  • Redesigned shop UI with dependency chains, resource icon chips, and state-based styling
Balance
  • Fall damage multiplier reduced from 10 to 8 (now that it costs HP instead of stamina)
  • Lava is now a massive HP hit (instant kill) instead of a binary death flag
  • Death screen now shows cause-specific messages (lava, fall, crumble)
v0.3.0 2026-04-03
New Shorkie breed, Pitty rework & placement fix
Added
  • New playable breed: Shorkie — treasure hunter with extra loot, same sprites as Terrier
  • Default dog names per breed (Diglet, Munchie, Tank, Scrappy, etc.) used when no name is entered
Changed
  • Labrador renamed to Pitty — now a speed demon with high move speed and jump height, mid stamina, and low dig speed
Fixed
  • Decorations must now touch the ground to be placed — no more floating decorations in the sky
  • Fixed mantling warping players up to distant ledges when climbing tall vertical tunnels
  • Scratch emote now recalls you to the surface with no resource penalty (4-minute cooldown)
v0.2.7 2026-04-03
Saved worlds sorted newest first
Changed
  • Load saved world menu now sorts worlds by most recently saved first
v0.2.6 2026-04-02
Improved mantling animation
Fixed
  • Mantling animation now pivots through the tile corner — sprite center aligns with the ledge edge at the midpoint for a natural "pulling over" motion
v0.2.5 2026-04-02
Fix idle zoom centering
Fixed
  • Dog no longer drifts to upper-left of screen during idle zoom — camera now correctly centers on player when zoomed
v0.2.4 2026-04-02
Fix sprite editor save not persisting
Fixed
  • Sprite editor saves now verify data was actually written to the database, with automatic retry on stale row IDs
  • Prevent saving before initial database load completes, avoiding race conditions that could overwrite edits
  • Handle edge case where sprite data arrives as a string instead of parsed JSON
v0.2.3 2026-04-02
Camera zoom polish
Fixed
  • Camera now stays centered on the dog during idle zoom transitions
  • Reduced pixel shimmer and anti-aliasing artifacts when zoomed
Changed
  • Increased idle sit delay from ~2s to ~3s (now configurable via IDLE_SIT_DELAY constant)
v0.2.2 2026-04-02
Idle camera zoom
Added
  • Camera slowly zooms in on your dog when they sit idle, and zooms back out when you start moving
v0.2.1 2026-04-02
Sprite animation & stamina fixes
Fixed
  • Jump animation now plays all frames by showing the jump sprite during the entire airborne phase, not just while moving upward
  • Breed picker on the title screen now waits for database sprites to load instead of briefly showing local fallback sprites
  • Horizontal digging now properly drains stamina — regen is suppressed while actively digging
v0.2.0 2026-04-02
Editable shop sprites
Added
  • Shop machine sprites (Decorations, Emotes, Upgrades) can now be edited in the sprite editor
  • Shop sprites persist to Supabase so edits carry over to the live game
  • New "Shops" mode in sprite editor with save/load support
v0.1.0 2026-04-02
Initial changelog
Added
  • Changelog page to track game updates
  • Single-player and multiplayer game modes
  • 4 playable breeds: Labrador, Dachshund, Husky, Terrier
  • Procedural world generation with caves, tunnels, and biomes
  • Shop system with decorations, emotes, and upgrades
  • In-browser sprite editor
  • BotW-style stamina and climbing system
